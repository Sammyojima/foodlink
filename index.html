FoodLink

Connecting surplus food to hungry mouths.

Register Login

How it works

  1. Donor posts surplus food (type, qty, expiry, pickup location).
  2. Nearby NGOs see available donations and claim them.
  3. Claimed donations are marked and tracked.

This starter project uses HTML/CSS/JS + PHP + MySQL (XAMPP/WAMP).
